Privacy Policy

CleanNote is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our markdown note-taking application.

1. Information We Collect

CleanNote stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. Data stored locally includes:

  • Notes and markdown content you create
  • Tags and organizational data
  • App preferences and settings
  • Purchase status for Pro subscription

2. Data Storage

All notes, tags, and settings are stored exclusively on your device using local database storage. We have no access to your notes and cannot retrieve them.

3. Third-Party Services

CleanNote uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store for in-app purchases ($1.99/month Pro subscription)
  • Anonymous crash reporting to improve app stability

We do not use any third-party advertising services.

4. In-App Purchases

Pro subscription purchases are processed entirely through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

CleanNote is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control. Delete all data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in device settings.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including encrypted local storage. Your Pro subscription status is stored using secure storage mechanisms.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App.

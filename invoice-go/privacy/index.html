Privacy Policy

InvoiceGo is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our invoice creation and management application.

1. Information We Collect

InvoiceGo stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. The types of data stored locally include:

  • Invoice data (client names, amounts, descriptions)
  • Client contact information you enter
  • App preferences and settings
  • Purchase status for Pro subscription

2. Data Storage

All your invoice data, client information, and app settings are stored exclusively on your device using encrypted local storage. We have no access to this data and cannot retrieve it.

3. Third-Party Services

InvoiceGo uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store for in-app purchases ($2.99/month Pro subscription)
  • Anonymous crash reporting to improve app stability

We do not use any third-party advertising services.

4. In-App Purchases

Pro subscription purchases are processed entirely through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

InvoiceGo is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through our App, please contact us.

6. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your data. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data through your device settings.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including encrypted local storage to protect your data. Your Pro subscription status is stored using secure storage mechanisms provided by the operating system.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App with an updated effective date.

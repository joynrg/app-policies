Privacy Policy

FocusForge is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our focus timer and habit tracking application.

1. Information We Collect

FocusForge stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. Data stored locally includes:

  • Focus session history and timer data
  • Habit tracking data and streaks
  • Custom sound preferences
  • App preferences and settings
  • Purchase status for Pro subscription

2. Data Storage

All focus sessions, habits, streaks, and settings are stored exclusively on your device using encrypted local storage. We have no access to this data.

3. Third-Party Services

FocusForge uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store for in-app purchases ($3.99/month Pro subscription)
  • Anonymous crash reporting to improve app stability

We do not use any third-party advertising services.

4. In-App Purchases

Pro subscription purchases are processed entirely through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

5. Notifications

FocusForge may send local notifications for timer completion and habit reminders. These are processed entirely on your device and do not transmit data externally.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

FocusForge is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Since all data is stored locally, you have complete control. Delete all data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in device settings.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including encrypted local storage. Your Pro subscription status is stored using secure storage mechanisms.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App.

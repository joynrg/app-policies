QuickQR Pro Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

QuickQR Pro (QR Code Scanner and Generator) is developed by Sei Won Lee. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to provide the app functionality:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous app usage statistics to improve performance
  • Purchase Data: In-app purchase records managed by Apple/Google
  • Camera Access: Used solely for QR code scanning, never recorded or transmitted
  • Device Info: Device type and OS version for compatibility

We do NOT collect personal information such as names, emails, or location data.

Data Storage

All QR code history and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store your content on external servers.

Third-Party Services

The app may use Apple App Store / Google Play Store for in-app purchases and Apple/Google crash reporting for stability improvements.

Children Privacy (COPPA)

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

GDPR and CCPA Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Since all data is stored locally, you can delete it by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
